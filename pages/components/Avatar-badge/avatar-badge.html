Examples

Basic Avatars

Avatar is available in 4 different sizes.You can use image or text initials for avatars.Include classes for different sizes in addition top the class avatar . avatar-xlg 5rem avatar-lg 4rem avatar-s 3rem avatar-xs 1.5rem

Status badge Avatars

Avatars can have badges to show their status as online , offline , disabled . And their shapes can be changed by changing their class.
